WELCOME to (S)IGNIS!



FIRE PREVENTION AND MANAGEMENT SERVICES​

Information



The main activity of our company is sensoring detecting early fires or hotspots and translate this data to an online platform for fire management. Our area of action focuses on the Valencian Community. This is due to the high concentration of fire events in the region, especially in the summer season. As we can see in the previous image, forest fires are a potentially dangerous risk in this region of the Iberian Peninsula. If we analyze the fire risk map of the Valencian Community (image below) we can see that practically the entire region has a high risk of occurrence. That is why the need arises to create a system that allows improving both the prevention and the management of forest fires. for this (S)IGNIS raises the idea of locating data collection sensors (humidity, temperature, etc.) that allow the study of the Valencian mountains in order to generate fire models and help the coordinating administrations to reduce the damages produced by a fire . The idea is to generate a system that allows instant field information to be instantia ted in addition to a later GIS analysis that allows a better management of the territory.
