WELCOME to (S)IGNIS!



FIRE PREVENTION AND MANAGEMENT SERVICES​

The main activity of our company is sensoring detecting early fires or hotspots and translate this data to an online platform for fire management. Our area of action focuses on the Valencian Community.

(S)IGNIS raises the idea of study of the Valencian mountains in order to generate fire models and help the coordinating administrations to reduce the damages produced by a fire . The idea is to generate a system that allows instant field information to be instantia ted in addition to a later GIS analysis that allows a better management of the territory. In the following section you can see a small summary about how the platform web work.

Information


The web platform have different functionalities depending on the user.

Free users:

In our home page, the free users can find information about our company, how to contact us and information about our products and services. Additionally, there are a viewer with fire layers, model of fire risk so you could see the risk of fire in each area.

Registered users:

If you are logged in, you will have access in the viewer to see more layers like a firewalls, you can edit this layer to insert, modify, or delete firewalls to later use that layer to create a fire risk model with current firewalls. In addition, you will have another tool to be able to compare two models of two different dates.
